## DESIGN GUIDELINES

### Color Palette

**Primary Colors:**
- Deep Purple: #6B2D7F (premium, professional)
- Vibrant Orange: #FF6B35 (energy, urgency)
- White: #FFFFFF (clarity, contrast)

**Secondary Colors:**
- Dark Gray: #2C3E50 (text, backgrounds)
- Light Gray: #ECF0F1 (subtle backgrounds)
- Accent Gold: #FFD700 (testimonials, highlights)
- Success Green: #4CAF50 (confirmations, checkmarks)
- Alert Red: #F44336 (warnings, X marks)

### Typography

**Headlines:** Bold, Sans-serif (Montserrat, Poppins, or similar)
**Body Text:** Regular, Sans-serif (Open Sans, Roboto)
**Emphasis:** Italic or color highlights

### Tone

- Professional yet friendly
- Modern and sleek
- African market appropriate
- Accessible and inclusive
