## NOTES FOR SALES AGENTS

**Tone & Personality:**
- Be friendly and conversational, not pushy
- Use "you" language (votre, vous)
- Match their energy level
- Be honest about limitations
- Share genuine enthusiasm for the product

**Active Listening:**
- Write down their concerns
- Address objections specifically (not generically)
- Ask follow-up questions to show you're listening
- Repeat back their concern before answering

**Timing:**
- Don't overwhelm with long messages - break it into 2-3 parts
- Give them time to respond - don't spam
- Follow up after 6 hours, 24 hours, 48 hours
- During business hours (9 AM - 8 PM Guinea time)

**Know Your Numbers:**
- 15k = Essentiel package
- 35k = Premium (most popular)
- 60k = Ultra
- 120k = Family
- 700+ = Total channels
- 50+ = Current customers in Guinea
- 24h = Free trial duration
- 5 min = Installation time

**Red Flags to Avoid:**
- Don't oversell or over-promise
- Don't badmouth competitors directly
- Don't share payment details in WhatsApp chat
- Don't give false legal guarantees
- Don't pressure excessively (3 follow-ups max)

**Green Lights to Close:**
- They ask "how much?"
- They ask technical questions (means interested)
- They ask about devices/compatibility (means ready)
- They agree to free trial
- They ask about payment methods
