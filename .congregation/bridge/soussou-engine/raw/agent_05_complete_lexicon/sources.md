## Language Characteristics (Important for Validation)

### Identification Markers
- **Pronouns**: Ntan/Itan/Ana/Whon'/Etan
- **Possessives**: M'ma/Akha
- **Negation**: m'ma/mu

### Must REJECT (Not Soussou)
- Pular (Fulfulde)
- Mandinka
- Wolof
- Bambara

### French Loanwords
French loanwords are NORMAL and should be ACCEPTED - Guinea was a French colony and French remains an official language.

### Grammar Features
- SOV word order
- No noun classes or gender
- Suffixing language
- Plural formed by adding -e
- Tonal language (tone marking optional in writing)
- Written in Latin, Arabic, or N'Ko scripts
