Yabura.
103.
Dawuda xa bɛɛti N xa Alatala tantu n nii birin na, n xa a xili sɛniyɛnxi matɔxɔ. 
N xa Alatala tantu n nii birin na, n naxa nɛɛmu a xa hinnɛ ma. 
A tan nan diɲɛma i xa yunubie ma, a i xa furee rayalan, 
a i ratanga faxɛ ma, a i rabilin a xa xanunteya nun a xa kinikini ra. 
A hinnɛma i ra i xa duniɲɛigiri kui, alako i man xa sɛnbɛ sɔtɔ i xa foriɲa kui alɔ sɛgɛ. 
Alatala ɲɛrɛma tinxinyi kui, a kiiti tinxinxi sama tɔɔrɔmixie bɛ. 
A bara a xa kira masen Annabi Munsa bɛ, a a xa kaabanakoee masen Isirayilakae bɛ. 
Alatala kinikinima mixie ma, a hinnɛ e ra, a mu xɔnɔma e ma mafuren mafuren, a xa xanunteya gbo. 
A mu mixie tɔɔrɔma tɛmui birin, a xa xɔnnanteya mu buma. 
A mu won ɲaxankatama won ma yunubi rabaxi ki ma, a mu won ma yunubi sare ragbilenma won ma. 
Koore itexi bɔxi xun ma ki naxɛ, a xa xanunteya fan gboxi na ki nɛ a yaragaaxuie bɛ. 
Sogetede makuyaxi sogegorode ra ki naxɛ, a won ma yunubie fan makuyaxi won na na ki nɛ. 
Babae kinikinima e xa die ma ki naxɛ, Alatala fan kinikinima a yaragaaxuie ma na ki nɛ, 
barima a a kolon won yailanxi se naxan na, a ratuxi a ma a bɛndɛ nan won na. 
Adamadie xa simaya maniya sansi ra naxan fugama fiili ma, 
kɔnɔ foye na bɔnbɔ a ra, a xarama nɛ, a lɔɛ. 
Kɔnɔ Alatala a yaragaaxuie xanuma nɛ abadan, a xa tinxinyi birama nɛ e bɔnsɔɛe fɔxɔ ra, 
naxee a xa saatɛ binyama, e a xa sɛriyɛ rabatu. 
Alatala bara a xa kibanyi dɔxɔ koore ma, a duniɲa birin yamari. 
Wo tan malekɛe, wo xa Alatala tantu, wo tan naxee sɛnbɛ gbo, wo tan naxee a rabatuma. 
Wo tan sɛnbɛma naxee na koore ma, wo xa Alatala tantu, wo tan naxee a waxɔnfe rabama. 
Wo tan birin naxee daaxi, wo xa Alatala tantu yire birin a xa mangɛya bun ma. N xa Alatala tantu n nii birin na.
