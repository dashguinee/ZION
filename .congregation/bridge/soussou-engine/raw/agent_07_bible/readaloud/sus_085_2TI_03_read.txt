Timote II.
3.
I xa a kolon, fe xɔrɔxɔɛe fama nɛ duniɲa lide waxati dɔnxɔɛ, 
barima adamadie birama e yɛtɛ waxɔnfe nan fɔxɔ ra, kɔbiri rafanma nɛ e ma, e e yɛtɛ igboma, e lama e yɛtɛ ra, e Ala rasɔtɔma, e e barimae matandima, e finsiriwaliya rabama, e mu Ala xa fe binyama, 
e mu kinikinima, e mu diɲɛma, e mixi mafalama, e xurutareɲa rabama, e e boore tɔɔrɔma, e mixi fanyi xɔnma, 
e mixi yanfama, e bogonma fee ma, e bɔɲɛ gbo, e waxɔnfe rafan e ma dangi Ala xa fe ra. 
A luxi nɛ alɔ e diinɛ nan nabatuma, kɔnɔ e bara e kobe raso a sɛnbɛ ra. I xa i makuya na mixi mɔɔlie ra. 
E soma denbayae kui, e fa ginɛ sɛnbɛtaree mabɛndun, naxee findixi yunubitɔɛe ra, naxee birama fe ɲaaxi mɔɔli birin fɔxɔ ra. 
Na ginɛ mɔɔli xaranma tɛmui birin, kɔnɔ e mu fahaamui sɔtɔma nɔndi ma. 
Na mixi naxee soma denbayae kui, e luma nɛ alɔ Yannɛsi nun Yanbɛrɛsi naxee Annabi Munsa matandi. Na mixie nɔndi fan matandima nɛ, e xaxili bara kana. E xa danxaniya xɔn mu kuyama. 
Kɔnɔ e mu sigama yare sɔnɔn, barima mixi birin fama nɛ e xa daxuɲa kolonde, alɔ Yannɛsi nun Yanbɛrɛsi xa fe nu kolonxi ki naxɛ. 
Kɔnɔ i tan, i bara n ma xaranyi kolon, n ɲɛrɛ ki, n ɲanige, n ma danxaniya, n ma diɲɛ, n ma xanunteya, n ma tunnabɛxi, 
n ma ɲaxankatɛ, a nun n ma tɔɔrɛ. Tɔɔrɛ mɔɔli mundun na, n mu naxan sɔtɔ Antiyɔki, Ikoniyon, nun Lisitire? Ɲaxankatɛ mundun mu n lixi naa? Marigi bara n nakisi na birin kui. 
Nɔndi na a ra a mixi naxan birin wama birafe Ala xa Mixi Sugandixi Isa xa sɛriyɛ fɔxɔ ra, a na kanyi tɔɔrɛ sɔtɔma nɛ. 
Kɔnɔ mixi ɲaaxie nun filankafuie xa fe kobie xun nan tun luma masa ra, e fa booree madaxu, e e yɛtɛ madaxu. 
I xa i xiri sɛriyɛ ra i naxan xaranxi, i danxaniyaxi naxan ma. I a kolon i na sɔtɔxi naxan na. 
Kabi i dimɛdi tɛmui, i Kitaabui sɛniyɛnxie kolon, naxee nɔma lɔnni fide i ma. Na lɔnni findima kisi nan na mixie bɛ, naxee danxaniyaxi Ala xa Mixi Sugandixi Isa ma. 
Kitaabui sɛniyɛnxi birin fatanxi Ala nan na. A fan xaranyi ra, a fan yaabi ra, a fan matinxinse ra, a fan tinxinyi masenfe ra, 
alako Ala xa mixi xa nɔ wali fanyi birin nabade.
