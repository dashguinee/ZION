Yabura.
20.
Dawuda xa bɛɛti bɛɛtibae xa mangɛ bɛ 
Alatala xa i yaabi i xa tɔɔrɛ kui. Yaxuba Marigi Ala xa i ratanga. 
Ala xa i mali kelife a xa yire sɛniyɛnxi, a xa i mali kelife Siyon geya fari. 
Ala xa i xa sɛrɛxɛ birin suxu, a xa ɲɛlɛxin i xa sɛrɛxɛ gan daaxie ra. Ɲɛngi rate. 
Ala xa i waxɔnfe fi i ma, a xa i xa wali sɔɔnɛya i bɛ. 
Won won xui itema ɲɛlɛxinyi kui. Won won ma tɔnxuma itema won Marigi Ala xili ra. Alatala xa i xa maxandi birin nasuxu. 
N a kolon a Alatala a xa mixi sugandixi rakisima nɛ. A a yaabima nɛ a sɛnbɛ ra kelife ra a xa yire sɛniyɛnxi. 
Nde lama a xa sɔɔri ragise ra, nde lama a xa soee ra, kɔnɔ won tan laxi won Marigi Alatala xili nan na. 
Soee tan birama nɛ, kɔnɔ won tan tixi tun. 
Alatala xa mangɛ rakisi, a xa won ma maxandi suxu.
