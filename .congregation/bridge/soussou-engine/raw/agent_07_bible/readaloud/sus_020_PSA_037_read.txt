Yabura.
37.
Dawuda gbe I naxa kɔntɔfili mixi ɲaaxie ra. I naxa tin lude alɔ e tan. 
E lisima nɛ mafuren alɔ ɲooge, e faxama nɛ alɔ sansie. 
I xa i taxu Alatala ra, i xa fe fanyi rabatu. I xa lu yi bɔxi ma, nɔndi xa findi i xa baloe ra. 
Alatala xa findi i waxɔnfe ra. Na kui a i waxɔnfe ragirima nɛ. 
I i xaxili ti Alatala ra, a birin taxu a ra. A tan nan fe ragirima. 
Ala fama i xa nɔndi makɛnɛnde alɔ a soge raminima ki naxɛ gɛɛsɛgɛ. A i xa xunnakeli makɛnɛnma alɔ soge naxan yanbama yanyi ra. 
I bɔɲɛ raxara Alatala ya i, i xa mamɛ ti, Ala na na. I naxa mixi ɲaaxi tɔɔnɛ hali a xa fe birin sɔɔnɛyaxi. 
I naxa xɔnɔ, i i bɔɲɛ magoro. Na tɔɔnɛ lu na, a mu fee sɔɔnɛyama i bɛ. 
Ala fama mixi ɲaaxie raɲɔnde, kɔnɔ naxee e xaxili tixi Ala ra, e fama yi bɔxi sɔtɔde kɛ ra. 
A mu buma mixi ɲaaxie xa lɔɛ duniɲa ma. E mu toma dɛdɛ. 
Naxee bɔɲɛe magoroxi, e tan nan fama yi bɔxi sɔtɔde kɛ ra, e lu bɔɲɛsa kui. 
Tinxintare tinxintɔɛ yanfama, a xɔnɔ a ma. 
Kɔnɔ Marigi yelema na mixi ɲaaxi ma, barima a a kolon a fama naxan sɔtɔde. 
Mixi ɲaaxie santidɛgɛma bama a tɛɛ i, e xali fan suxu, alako e xa misikiinɛ nun setare tɔɔrɔ, e xa tinxintɔɛ kɔn naxaba. 
Kɔnɔ e tan nan fama faxade e yɛtɛ xa santidɛgɛma saabui ra. E xa xalie fama girade. 
Se xuri naxan na tinxintɔɛ yi ra, a fisa mixi ɲaaxie xa bannaya bɛ, 
barima Alatala e sɛnbɛ kanama nɛ, a fa tinxintɔɛ mali. 
Alatala ɲɛngi saxi mixi fanyi xɔn. E kɛ buma nɛ abadan. 
E mu yaagima tɔɔrɛ tɛmui, e lugama nɛ kaamɛ waxati. 
Kɔnɔ mixi ɲaaxie faxama nɛ. Alatala yaxuie luma nɛ alɔ fiili, naxan sɛxɛ lisima, a fa gan. 
Mixi ɲaaxi doni tongoma nɛ, kɔnɔ e mu a fima. Mixi fanyi tan mixie kima a xa fonisireya kui. 
Alatala xa barakatɔɛe yi bɔxi sɔtɔma nɛ kɛ ra, kɔnɔ Ala naxan dankama, na kanyi fama halakide. 
Naxan ɲɛrɛ ki rafan Alatala ma, a na kanyi malima nɛ a xa duniɲɛigiri kui. 
Hali a sa dinkon, a mu birama, barima a bɛlɛxɛ na Alatala yi ra. 
Kabi n dimɛdi tɛmui, han n xɛmɔxi ra, n mu tinxintɔɛ keren toxi Ala naxan nabɛɲinxi, naxan xa die taami makulama. 
Tinxintɔɛ ki tima, a mixie donima. A xa die barakɛ sɔtɔma nɛ. 
I xa fe kobi lu na, i xa fe fanyi raba. Na kui i simaya xɔnkuye sɔtɔma nɛ. 
Sɛriyɛ rafan Alatala ma. A mu a xa hinnɛntee rabɛɲinma. A e kantama nɛ abadan. Kɔnɔ mixi ɲaaxie bɔnsɔɛ fama nɛ halakide. 
Tinxintɔɛe fama nɛ yi bɔxi sɔtɔde kɛ ra, e lu be abadan. 
Tinxintɔɛ lɔnni nan masenma, sɛriyɛ nan minima a dɛ kui. 
A Marigi Ala xa sɛriyɛ na a bɔɲɛ kui, a mu birama kira xɔn. 
Mixi ɲaaxi tinxintɔɛ yanfama, a xa a faxa. 
Kɔnɔ Alatala mu tinxintɔɛ luma a yaxuie sagoe. A mu tinma e xa a tɔɔɲɛgɛ kiiti tɛmui. 
I xaxili ti Alatala ra, a xa kira suxu, alako a xa i rakeli, i xa yi bɔxi sɔtɔ kɛ ra. Na tɛmui i mixi ɲaaxie toma nɛ e halakixi. 
Tɛmui dangixi n bara sɛnbɛma ɲaaxi nde to. A xungbo alɔ wuri tofanyi. 
Kɔnɔ n to dangi a yire a firin nde, n naxa a li a xa fe xuben. N naxa a fen, kɔnɔ n mu a to. 
I xa mixi fanyi nan mato, tinxintɔɛ nan igbɛ. Na mixi mɔɔli findima barakatɔɛ nan na. 
Kɔnɔ yunubitɔɛe tan fama nɛ sɔntɔde, e bɔnsɔɛ birin fa halaki. 
Alatala tinxintɔɛe rakisima nɛ, a findi e xa makantama ra e xa tɔɔrɛ kui. 
A e malima nɛ, a e ratanga. A e ratanga mixi ɲaaxie ma, a e rakisi, barima e yigiyaxi a tan nan ma.
