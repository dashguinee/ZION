Taalie.
6.
N ma di, xa i i yɛtɛ taanixi i boore nde bɛ, i findixi hamilu ra a bɛ e nun mixi gbɛtɛ tagi, 
xa i i yɛtɛ raxɛtɛnxi i xa wɔyɛnyi nde ra, xa i i yɛtɛ xirixi na laayidi mɔɔli ra, 
n ma di, i xa fe birin naba i yɛtɛ raminide a kui, xa na mu a ra i luma nɛ i boore sagoe. Siga, i i felen i boore bɛ, i xa a mayandi. 
I naxa xi de, na raba keren na. 
I i yɛtɛ ratanga yi fe mɔɔli ma, alɔ xeli a yɛtɛ ratangama koyinma bɛlɛxɛ ma ki naxɛ, alɔ xɔni fan a yɛtɛ ratangama gantanyi ma ki naxɛ. 
Tunnaxɔnɛ, siga dondoli yire. Marasi fen a ra, alako i xa findi xaxilima ra. 
Mangɛ mu na dondoli bɛ, kuntigi nun yarerati fan mu a bɛ, 
kɔnɔ a walima sogofure tɛmui, alako a xa baloe sɔtɔ a naxan donma ɲɛmɛ ra. 
I tan tunnaxɔnɛ, i i sama han mun tɛmui? I kelima xixɔli ma tɛmui mundun? 
Xixɔli na i suxu, i na kinsɔn, i na i sa, 
setareɲa fan fama nɛ i suxude tɛrɛnna ra, alɔ muɲɛti mixi tɛrɛnnama ki naxɛ. Kaamɛ i suxuma nɛ, alɔ sɔɔri mixi suxuma ki naxɛ. 
Fuyante mu tinxin, a wule raɲɛrɛma a dɛ i nɛ. 
A luma a ya magira ra, a nu mixi makebentin a sanyie nun a bɛlɛxɛe ra wule tɔnxuma ra. 
Fe ɲaaxi na a xaxili kui, a gere masoma tɛmui birin. 
Na kui kasarɛ fama nɛ a suxude, a a xun nakana kerenyi ra. 
Fe senni na Alatala naxee xɔnxi, a mu tinma nee ra fefe ma: 
yɛtɛ igboe xa mixi mato ki, wule minima dɛ naxan kui, bɛlɛxɛ naxan nii bama fufafu ra, 
bɔɲɛ naxan fe tinxintare maɲɔxunma, sanyi naxee e gima sigafe ra fe kobi rabade, 
seede naxan wule falama, a nun mixi naxan ngaxakerenmae tagi isoma. 
N ma di, i baba xa sɛriyɛ ragata, i naxa tondi i nga xa marasi ra. 
E xa lu i bɔɲɛ kui tɛmui birin, e xiri i kɔn ma. 
E i raɲɛrɛma nɛ i xa duniɲɛigiri kui, e i makanta i xa sade ma, e i rasi i keli tɛmui. 
Na sɛriyɛ findixi lanpui nan na, na xaranyi kira masenma nɛ i bɛ, na marasi i xun tima simaya kira nan xɔn ma. 
E i ratangama ginɛ kobi ma, nun xɔɲɛ ginɛ dɛ iɲɔxunxi ma. 
I naxa wa na ginɛ xɔn ma a xa tofanyi ma, i naxa tin a xa nɔ i ra a yae ra. 
Langoe ginɛ naxan mu na xɛmɛ taa, a mixi findima setare nan na, kɔnɔ ginɛ yɛnɛla naxan dɔxɔxi xɛmɛ nde xɔn, a mixi faxama nɛ. 
Mixi nde nɔma tɛ tongode, a a sa a yuba kui, a fa lu a xa donma mu gan? 
Mixi nde nɔma a ɲɛrɛde tɛ wole tagi, a fa lu a sanyi mu gan? 
Na maniyama nɛ alɔ xɛmɛ naxan yabuma ginɛ xɛmɛ taa idɔxɔɛ ra. Xɛmɛ yo din na ginɛ ra, a fama ɲaxankatɛ nan sɔtɔde. 
Mixi mu yoma muɲɛti ma, naxan muɲɛ tixi a xa kaamɛ ma, 
kɔnɔ a na suxu fo a xa muɲafe sare fi, hali na findi a xa naafuli birin na. 
Kɔnɔ xaxilitare nan tun yɛnɛ rabama ginɛ ra. A a xun nakana na fe mɔɔli ra. 
A fe xɔrɔxɔɛ sɔtɔma nɛ yaagi kui, naxan mu gbilenma a fɔxɔ ra sɔnɔn, 
barima tɔɔnɛ xɛmɛ bɔɲɛ ratema nɛ a ɲaaxi ra. Ginɛ kanyi mu kinikinima a gbe ɲɔxɔɛ lɔxɔɛ. 
A bɔɲɛ mu goroma kɔbiri ra. Hali i a gbegbe fi a ma, a mu a rasuxuma.
