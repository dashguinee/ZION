Taalie.
2.
N ma di, xa i n ma masenyie suxu, xa i n ma sɛriyɛ rabatu, 
xa i i tuli mati lɔnni ra, xa nɔndi rafan i bɔɲɛ ma, 
xa i xaxili fanyi fen, xa i nɔndi maxili, 
xa i a fen alɔ gbeti, xa i a fen a fanyi ra alɔ bannaya fenma ki naxɛ, 
na kui, i Alatala xa yaragaaxui kolonma nɛ, i Ala xa fe fahaamuma nɛ, 
barima Alatala nan lɔnni fima, xaxili fanyi nun nɔndi minima a kɛrɛ i. 
A fe fanyi gbegbe ragatama tinxintɔɛe bɛ. A nɔndi falɛ makantama. 
A mixi tinxinxi makantama, a a xanuntenyie ratangama kira xɔn ma. 
Na kui, i nɔndi nun tinxinyi kolonma nɛ, kira tinxinxi naxee mixi tima fe fanyi ma. 
Lɔnni fama i bɔɲɛ nan ma, fe kolonyi ɲɔxunma i bɛ, 
xaxili fanyi i ratangama, nɔndi i makantama, 
a i ratangama kira ɲaaxi ma, a i ratangama wɔyɛn kobi falɛ ma, 
naxee gbilenma tinxinyi kira fɔxɔ ra, naxee ɲɛrɛma dimi kui, 
fe ɲaaxi rafanxi naxee ma, naxee ɲɛlɛxinxi kobiɲa ra, 
naxee xa kira mu tinxin, naxee ɲɛrɛ ki mu fan. 
Lɔnni i ratangama nɛ ginɛ ɲaaxi ma, langoe ginɛ xa dɛ iɲɔxunyi ma, 
naxan a xa xɛmɛ singe finsiriwalima, naxan nɛɛmuma a Marigi Ala xa saatɛ ma. 
A xa wali kobi a rabirama nɛ, a ɲɛrɛ ki a xanin aligiyama. 
Mixi naxee birama a fɔxɔ ra, e mu gbilenma sɔnɔn, e mu kisi sɔtɔma. 
A lanma i xa i ɲɛrɛ mixi fanyie nan ma kira ra, i xa tinxintɔɛe xa kira suxu. 
Mixi tinxinxie luma nɛ yi bɔxi ma, mixi hagigɛe mu kelima be, 
kɔnɔ mixi ɲaaxie tan ɲɔnma nɛ yi bɔxi ma, tinxintaree kerima nɛ.
