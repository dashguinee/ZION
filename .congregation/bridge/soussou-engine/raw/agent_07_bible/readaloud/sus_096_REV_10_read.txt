Waxayi.
10.
N naxa malekɛ sɛnbɛma gbɛtɛ to goro ra kelife koore nuxui kui. Senkui nu na a xunyi rabilinyi, a yatagi nu yanbama alɔ soge, a sanyie nu maniyaxi tɛ nan na. 
Buki lanmadi nu na a bɛlɛxɛ, a rabixi. Malekɛ yirefanyi sanyi nu tixi baa fari, boore fan nu tixi xare ma. 
A naxa a xui ramini sɛnbɛ ra, naxan maniya yɛtɛ xui ra. Galanyi solofere naxa a xui ratin. 
Na galanyi solofere xui to mini, n nu wama na sɛbɛfe, kɔnɔ xui nde naxa keli koore ma, a a fala n bɛ, «Galanyi solofere xa masenyi nɔxun, i naxa a sɛbɛ.» 
Malekɛ naxan sanyi nu tixi baa nun xare fari, a naxa a yirefanyi bɛlɛxɛ itala koore ma, 
a fa a kali Ala ra naxan ɲiɲɛ a ra abadan, naxan koore, bɔxi, nun baa daa, a nun e kui se birin. A naxa a masen a waxati mu fa dugundife sɔnɔn. 
Malekɛ solofere nde na a xa sara fe tɛmui naxɛ, Ala gundo fama nɛ kamalide alɔ a nu bara a masen a xa konyi namiɲɔnmɛe bɛ ki naxɛ. 
Na xui naxan nu bara wɔyɛn n bɛ kelife koore ma, na man naxa a masen n bɛ, «Siga, i sa buki lanmadi rabixi tongo naxan na malekɛ bɛlɛxɛ, malekɛ naxan sanyi tixi baa nun xare fari.» 
N naxa siga na malekɛ yire, n fa a maxandi a xa na buki lanmadi so n yi ra. A naxa a fala n bɛ, «I xa a tongo, a don. A xɔnɛ rasoma nɛ i furi, kɔnɔ a ɲɔxunma nɛ i dɛ kui alɔ kumi.» 
N naxa na buki lanmadi tongo malekɛ bɛlɛxɛ, n naxa a don. A nu ɲɔxun n dɛ kui alɔ kumi, kɔnɔ a to n furi kui li, a naxa findi xɔnɛ ra. 
Na tɛmui a naxa a fala n bɛ, «A lanma i man xa wɔyɛn Ala xili ra ɲamanɛe xa fe ra, sie xa fe ra, xui gbɛtɛ falɛe xa fe ra, nun mangɛ gbegbe xa fe ra.»
