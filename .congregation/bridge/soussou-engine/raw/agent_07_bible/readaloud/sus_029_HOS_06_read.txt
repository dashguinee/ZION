Annabi Hose.
6.
Wo fa be, won xa gbilen Alatala ma! A bara won tɔɔrɔ, kɔnɔ yakɔsi a won nayalanma nɛ. A bara won bɔnbɔ, kɔnɔ yakɔsi a won ma fie maxirima nɛ. 
Xi firin na dangi, a fama nɛ won nayalande, a xi saxan nde a won nakeli, won fa lu a sɛɛti ma. 
Won xa Alatala fen, won xa a kolon. Ala fafe mu kanama. Lɔxɔ yo lɔxɔ kuye ibama, ɲɛmɛ birin tunɛ fama. Ala fan mu tondima fade won yire. 
«N munse rabama i tan na, Efirami? N munse rabama i ra, Yudaya? I xa hinnɛ n mabiri, luxi nɛ alɔ xini naxan goroma gɛɛsɛgɛ, kɔnɔ beenun soge xa xɔnɔ, a ɲɔn. 
N wo gerema namiɲɔnmɛe xa masenyie ra na nan ma, n wo faxama nɛ n ma masenyie ra. N ma kiitie fiixɛ alɔ naiyalanyi. 
Hinnɛ rafan n ma dangife sɛrɛxɛ ra, Ala kolonfe dangi sɛrɛxɛ gan daaxie ra. 
E bara n ma saatɛ kana, alɔ adamadie darixi a ra ki naxɛ. E bara n yanfa. 
Mixi ɲaaxi wuya Galadi, faxɛtie bara gbo naa. 
Sɛrɛxɛdubɛe baganma mixi ma Sikemi kira xɔn ma alɔ muɲɛtie. Na faxɛ tife, fe ɲaaxi na a ra. 
N bara fe ɲaaxie to Isirayilakae tagi: Efirami bara langoeɲa raba, Isirayila bara nɔxɔ. 
Yudaya, n na gɛ n ma ɲama ragbilende konyiya kui, n wo fan ɲaxankatama nɛ.»
