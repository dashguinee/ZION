Yabura.
95.
Won xa bɛɛti ba Marigi bɛ sɛɛwɛ ra, won xa won xui ite won nakisima sɛnbɛma bɛ. 
Won xa a matɔxɔ, won xa a matɔxɔɛ ba a bɛ bɛɛtie ra, 
barima Alatala, Ala xungbe na a ra, naxan findixi mangɛ xungbe ra sɛnbɛmae birin xun ma. 
Yire tilinxi na a bɛlɛxɛ. Geya xuntagie findixi a gbe nan na, 
alɔ baa nun bɔxi a naxee daaxi a bɛlɛxɛ ra. 
Wo fa, won xa suyidi, won xa won igoro, won xa won xinbi sin Alatala ya i naxan won daa, 
barima a tan nan won Marigi Ala ra. Won findixi a xa ɲama nan na a naxan dɛmadonma, alɔ xuruse dɛmadonyi mɛɛnima gɔɔrɛ ma ki naxɛ. Wo xa a xui mɛ to. 
Wo naxa wo bɔɲɛe raxɔrɔxɔ alɔ naxan nabaxi Meriba, alɔ naxan nabaxi Masa gbengberenyi ma tɛmui dangixi. 
Wo benbae n mato nɛ na lɔxɔɛ, hali e to nu bara n wali fɔxi to. 
Na bɔnsɔɛ xa fe naxa raɲaaxu n ma ɲɛ tongo naani bun ma. N naxa a fala, «Ɲama xurutare nan yi ki, naxan mu n waxɔnfe kolon.» 
Na kui n naxa n kali n ma xɔnɛ kui, «E mu soma n ma malabude kui fefe ma!»
