# Common Soussou Phrases

## Greetings

| Soussou | English | French |
|---------|---------|--------|
| I ni khere | Hello/Peace to you | Bonjour/Paix sur toi |
| I khere ma | Peace to you | Paix sur toi |
| Tana ma seri | Good morning | Bonjour (matin) |
| Wura ma seri | Good afternoon | Bon apres-midi |
| Koe ma seri | Good evening | Bonsoir |
| Ina wama? | Are you okay? | Tu vas bien? |
| Ntan wama | I'm fine | Je vais bien |
| Tan dima | Thank you | Merci |
| Awa | Yes | Oui |
| Ayi | No | Non |

## Common Responses

| Soussou | English | French |
|---------|---------|--------|

## Useful Compound Phrases

| Soussou | English |
|---------|---------|
| N'na fafé, itan go? | I'm coming, what about you? |
| Ntan itan khafé comprendfé gbo | I understand your situation a lot |
| Whon' fafé whonma bateau fafé | We're coming, our boat is coming |
| Ana m'ma fafé, akha woto yite | He's not coming, but his car is here |
| Ntan m'ma no'mma fafé | I can't come |
| Etan itan khafé comprendfé gbo | They understand a lot because of you |
