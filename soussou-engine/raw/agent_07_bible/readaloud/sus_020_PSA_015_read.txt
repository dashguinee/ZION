Yabura.
15.
Dawuda xa bɛɛti Alatala, nde nɔma lude i xa salide kui? Nde nɔma sabatide i xa geya sɛniyɛnxi fari? 
Mixi naxan ɲɛrɛma tinxinyi kira xɔn ma, naxan fe fanyi rabama, naxan nɔndi falama a bɔɲɛ fiixɛ ra. 
A mu mixi mafalama, a mu fe ɲaaxi rabama a boore ra, a man mu a rayaagima. 
A a makuyama mixi ra naxan mu rafan Ala ma, kɔnɔ a mixi binyama naxan gaaxuma Ala ya ra. Hali a tɔɔrɔma, a mu a xa marakali masarama. 
A mu mixi donima kɔbiri ra alako a xa geeni sɔtɔ. A mu kɔbiri rasuxuma a xa wule sa mixi xun ma. Yi mixi mɔɔli mu dinkonma abadan.
