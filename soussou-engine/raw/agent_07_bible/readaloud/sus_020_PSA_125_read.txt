Yabura.
125.
Biyaasilae xa bɛɛti nde Naxee e xaxili tima Alatala ra, e sɛnbɛ sɔtɔma nɛ alɔ Siyoni geya naxan mu sɛrɛnma, a bu abadan. 
Darisalamu rabilinxi geyae ra ki naxɛ, Alatala a xa ɲama fan nabilinxi na ki nɛ abadan. 
Mangasanyi ɲaaxi mu tinxintɔɛ xa bɔxi yamarima, alako tinxintɔɛ bɛlɛxɛ naxa lan fe ɲaaxi. 
Alatala, hinnɛ mixi fanyie ra, naxee bɔɲɛ fiixɛ, 
kɔnɔ i tan Alatala, i xa tinxintaree keri e yuge ma. Ala xa bɔɲɛsa lu Isirayila bɔxi ma.
