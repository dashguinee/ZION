Annabi Esayi.
35.
Ɲɛlɛxinyi xa lu gbengberen yire, sɛɛwɛ xa lu wulai alɔ sansi fugaxie yire. 
Sansi tofanyie xa lu naa, sɛɛwɛ xui xa ite. A xa lu alɔ Liban fɔtɔnyi, a xa tofan alɔ Karemele geya nun Sarɔn yire. Won Marigi Alatala xa nɔrɛ toma nɛ naa a fanyi ra. 
Wo tan naxee bara gaaxu han wo bɛlɛxɛ mu se suxuma, wo xinbi fa sɛrɛn, wo sɛnbɛ xa so. 
Wo a fala mixie bɛ naxee bara gaaxu, «Wo xa limaniya, hali wo mu gaaxu! Wo Marigi Ala bara fa wo gbe ɲɔxɔde. A fama wo rakiside a sɛnbɛ ra.» 
Na tɛmui, dɔnxuie ya rabɔɔma nɛ, tulixɔrie fe mɛma nɛ. 
Mabɛnyie tuganma nɛ alɔ xeli, boboe fan sɛɛwɛ xui raminima nɛ, barima xuree minima nɛ gbengberenyi ma. 
Bɔxi maxaraxi mafindima nɛ baa ra, dulonyi minima nɛ gbengberen yire. Wulai baree xɔnyi bara findi ye yire ra. 
Kira mabama naa naxan xili falama, «Kira Sɛniyɛnxi.» Mixi sɛniyɛntare mu dangima mɛnni, fo naxan lanma a xa dangi na. Xaxilitaree mu tima na kira xɔn ma. 
Yɛtɛe nun burunyi sube mu toma naa, fo lasiri mixi. 
Alatala naxee xɔrɛyaxi, nee nan gbilenma e xɔnyi na kira xɔn ma. E soma nɛ Siyoni sɛɛwɛ ra naxan mu ɲɔnma. E ɲɛlɛxinma nɛ tun. Sunnunyi nun tɔɔrɛ bama nɛ e ma abadan.
