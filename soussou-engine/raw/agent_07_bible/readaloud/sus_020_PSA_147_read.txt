Yabura.
147.
Wo Alatala matɔxɔ. A lanma Alatala xa matɔxɔ bɛɛti ra. Fe fanyi na a ra. 
Alatala na gbilenfe Darisalamu ti ra, a Isirayila xa mixi rafama nɛ kelife konyiya kui, 
a tɔɔrɔmixie ralimaniyama, a e rayalanma e xa fure kui. 
Alatala tunbuie kɔnti kolon, a e birin xilima e xili ra. 
Won Marigi Alatala sɛnbɛ gbo, dan mu na a xa fahaamui ma. 
Alatala mixi magoroxi bun tima, kɔnɔ a yɛtɛ igboe xa fe kanama. 
Wo xa bɛɛti ba Alatala bɛ tantui ra, wo xa bɛɛti ba Ala bɛ kɔrae xui ra. 
A tan nan nuxui raminima koore ma, a tunɛ rafa bɔxi ma, a sansie rasoli geyae fari. 
A xurusee rabaloma, a donse fima xaxa yɔrɛe ma, e na e xui ramini tɛmui naxɛ. 
Soe sɛnbɛ mu findixi fefe ra Ala bɛ. Xɛmɛ xa sɛnbɛ mu findixi fefe ra Ala bɛ. 
Ala yaragaaxuiya nan nafanma a ma. Ala wama mixi xɔn naxan xaxili tixi a xa xanunteya ra. 
Darisalamu, i xa Alatala matɔxɔ. Siyoni, i xa Ala tantu, 
barima a tan nan i xa naadɛ makantama, a tan nan barakɛ sama i xa ɲama ma. 
A bɔɲɛsa fima i xa bɔxi ma, a donse fanyi fima i ma. 
Alatala duniɲa yamarima, a a waxɔnfe rayensenma mafuren mafuren. 
A balabalanyi ragoroma nɛ alɔ gɛsɛ fute, a na rayensen alɔ tɛ xube. 
A ragoro bɔxi ma, nde nɔma tide na xinbeli ya ra? 
A na a xui ramini e ma, e xunuma nɛ, a na foye rafa, balabalanyi man findima nɛ ye ra. 
Alatala bara Annabi Yaxuba rakolon a xa masenyi ra, a bara a xa sɛriyɛ nun a xa yaamarie masen Isirayila bɛ. 
A mu na mɔɔli ragirixi si gbɛtɛ yo bɛ. Nee luma Ala xa sɛriyɛe kolontareya nan kui. Wo xa Alatala matɔxɔ!
