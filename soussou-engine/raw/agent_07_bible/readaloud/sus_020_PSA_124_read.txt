Yabura.
124.
Biyaasilae xa bɛɛti nde Dawuda naxan sɛbɛ Isirayila ɲama xa a fala: Xa Alatala mu lu won sɛɛti ma nu, 
xa Alatala mu won mali gere kui nu, 
na gerefae bara won xun nakana e xa xɔnɛ ra nu, 
alɔ banbaranyi dinma xure ma ki naxɛ, 
a fa mixie madula ye xɔɔra sɛnbɛ ra. 
Tantui na Alatala bɛ, naxan mu tin e xa nɔ won na. 
Won bara kisi alɔ xɔni naxan kisixi gantanyi ra. Gantanyi bara kana, yɛlɛ bara bɔɔ a ra, won bara e lu naa. 
Won ma dɛmɛri na Alatala xili nan na, naxan koore nun bɔxi daaxi.
