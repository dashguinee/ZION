Annabi Esayi.
53.
Nde laxi won ma masenyi ra? Nde Alatala sɛnbɛ toxi? 
A xa konyi lu nɛ alɔ sansi xuri, naxan tixi bɔxi xare ma. A to ki mu nu rayabu, a xa tofanyi mu gbo. 
Mixi raɲaaxuxi nan nu a ra ɲama mu tinxi naxan na. Tɔɔrɔmixi nan nu a ra, naxan darixi ɲaxankatɛ ra. Mixie mu tinxi e ya tide a bɛ. E nu yo a ma, e mu tide yo fixi a ma. 
A bara won ma tɔɔrɛ findi a gbe ra, a bara won ma kɔntɔfili findi a gbe ra. Won tan bara a maɲɔxun a Ala nan a dankaxi, a a xa tɔɔrɛ fatanxi Ala nan na. 
Kɔnɔ a sɔxɔ nɛ won ma yunubie xa fe ra, a butuxun won ma ɲaaxuɲa xa fe ra. A ɲaxankatɛ sɔtɔ nɛ alako won xa yalanyi sɔtɔ, a xa fie findixi kisi nan na won bɛ. 
Won birin nu lɔɛxi nɛ alɔ xuruse naxee yensenma wula i, kankan naxa siga a xa kira xɔn ma. Kɔnɔ Alatala bara won ma yunubie kote dɔxɔ a xun. 
Tɔɔrɛ nun fe xɔrɔxɔɛ to a li, a mu a dɛ rabi hali keren. A lu nɛ alɔ yɛxɛɛ naxan xaninfe a faxade. A a sabari nɛ tɔɔrɛ kui, alɔ yɛxɛɛ xabe maxaba tɛmui. 
A to gɛ kiiti tinxintare sɔtɔde, a naxa faxa. Nde nɔ a bade duniɲa mixie yi ra? A ɲaxankataxi n ma ɲama xa yunubie nan ma fe ra. 
E nu wama a fure ragatafe mixi ɲaaxie nan ya ma, kɔnɔ a raɲɔnyi a ragata mixi bannaxie nan ya ma, barima a mu mixi tɔɔrɔ, a mu mixi madaxu. 
Alatala bara natɛ tongo a xa a ɲaxankata tɔɔrɛ ra, kɔnɔ sɛrɛxɛ na ba yunubie xa fe ra, a fama nɛ a bɔnsɔɛ tode, a bu abadan. Alatala waxɔnfe rakamalima a tan nan saabui ra. 
A na gɛ a xa wali rabade, a fama nɛ sɛɛwade. «N ma konyi tinxinxi ɲama findima nɛ tinxintɔɛe ra, a e xun sara e xa yunubie xa fe ra. 
N tide xungbe soma nɛ a yi ra, a xunnakeli sare sɔtɔ sɛnbɛ ra, a to faxa matandilae yi ra, a nu bara a yɛtɛ fi sɛrɛxɛ ra mixi gbegbe xa yunubie xa fe ra.»
