Taalie.
7.
N ma di, n ma wɔyɛnyi ragata i bɔɲɛ kui, n ma sɛriyɛ xa lu i sondonyi ma. 
N ma sɛriyɛ suxu alako i xa simaya sɔtɔ, n ma xaranyi xa rafan i ma alɔ i ya firinyie. 
N ma sɛriyɛ xiri i bɛlɛxɛsolee ra, a sɛbɛ i sondonyi ma. 
Lɔnni xa findi i maaginɛ ra, xaxili fanyi xa findi i bari mixi ra. 
N ma sɛriyɛ i ratangama nɛ langoe ma, e i ratangama nɛ yɛnɛla xɛmɛ taa idɔxɔɛ ma. 
Lɔxɔɛ nde, n nu na n ma banxi kui, n tande matoma wundɛri ra. 
N naxa xaxilitare nde to, sɛgɛtala naxan xa fahaamui mu gbo. 
A naxa kira suxu naxan dangima langoe ginɛ xa banxi dɛ ra. A nu bara a xun ti mɛnni ra. 
Nunmare tɛmui nan nu a ra, kɔɛ nu bara so, kuye nu bara ifɔɔrɔ. 
Ginɛ nde naxa sa a ralan, a maxirixi langoe ginɛ ki, a nu wama na xɛmɛ madaxufe nɛ. 
Ginɛ murutaxi nan a ra, naxan wɔyɛn xui ite, a mixi matandi. 
A ɲɛrɛma taa kui xɛmɛ fenfe ra. 
A naxa na xɛmɛ suxu, a a masunbu, a a fala a bɛ yɛtɛ igboɲa ra, 
«N laayidi tongo nɛ Ala bɛ, n bara na rakamali sɛrɛxɛ ra to. 
N minixi i fende na nan ma. Yakɔsi n bara i to. 
Misira sade dugi maɲingixi na n sade ma, 
a nun labundɛ xiri fanyi mɔɔli birin na. 
Fa be, won xa yɛnɛ raba han gɛɛsɛgɛ, won xa won waxɔnyi birin naba, 
barima n ma xɛmɛ mu na banxi, a sigaxi biyaasi xɔnkuye. 
A bara kɔbiri gbegbe xanin a xun ma, a mu fama fo yi kike ɲɔn.» 
A naxa nɔ na xɛmɛ ra a xa kɔɔta saabui ra, a naxa nɔ a ra a xa dɛ iɲɔxunyi ra. 
Xɛmɛ naxa bira a fɔxɔ ra keren na, alɔ ninge naxan sigafe ninge faxade, alɔ xeli naxan a sanyi rasofe yɛlɛ kui, alɔ xɔni naxan tife gantanyi ma, han tanbɛ fa a bɔɲɛ sɔxɔ, a faxa, a mu a kolon a xa simaya danma mɛnni nɛ. 
Yakɔsi n ma die, wo wo tuli mati n na, wo n ma masenyi ramɛ. 
I bɔɲɛ naxa i madaxu i xa bira na ginɛ mɔɔli fɔxɔ ra, i naxa i ɲɛrɛ a xa kira ra, 
barima a bara mixi gbegbe faxa, a bara sɛnbɛmae yati rabira. 
A xa banxi findixi faxɛ kira nan na, mixi goroma aligiyama dɛnnaxɛ ra.
