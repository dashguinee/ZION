Ayuba.
8.
Bilidada Suuxaka naxa a masen, 
«I yi wɔyɛnyi mɔɔli falama han tɛmui mundun? I xa wɔyɛnyi luxi nɛ alɔ foye. 
Ala nɔma nɔndi mafindide wule ra? Ala Sɛnbɛ Kanyi nɔma tinxinyi ifude? 
Xa i xa die yunubi nan nabaxi a ra, a e ɲaxankataxi na nan ma. 
Xa i tan Ala fenma, xa i Ala Sɛnbɛ Kanyi makulama, 
xa yunubi mu na i ma, xa i tinxin, Ala a ɲɛngi sama nɛ i xɔn ma, a i rasabati i xa tinxinyi kui. 
I harige dɔnxɔɛ fama dangide na singe ra.» 
«Fahaamui fen won babae ra, i xa xaxili sɔtɔ nee benbae fan na. 
Won tan mu buxi duniɲa ma, xaxili xɔnkuye mu na won bɛ. 
E mu fahaamui fima xɛ i ma? E e xaxili xa masenyie tima i bɛ yi ki: 
Wofiri bulama xare ma? Xure i baare findima a ra ye mu dɛnnaxɛ? 
Hali a na a bili ma, a mu xabaxi, a lisima mafuren dangi ɲooge birin na. 
Na nan fama rabade mixie ra, naxee nɛɛmuma Ala ma. Tinxintare xaxili tide ɲɔnma na ki nɛ. 
A xaxili tide bara kana, alɔ mixi naxan a kilɔnma sayɛlɛ tɛɛ ra. 
A na a kilɔn na ra, a kanama nɛ, a kanyi bira. 
A luma alɔ sansi, ye naxan kui rafexi, a a salonyi iyan soge linlinyi ya ma laakɔɛ xun ma, 
a a sankee sɔti gɛmɛ malanxi longori ra. 
Xa i na sansi tala naa, a lisima nɛ, 
a mu fanma mɛnni sɔnɔn. Sansi gbɛtɛ man fa bula a nu na dɛnnaxɛ. 
Ala mu tinxintɔɛ rawɔlɛma, a mu tinxintare sɛnbɛ soma. 
A gbe mu luxi a xa i rasɛɛwa, i xa matɔxɔɛ falɛ ra i fama naxan tide. 
I xɔnmixie fama lude yaagi kui, e xɔnyie fa bira.»
