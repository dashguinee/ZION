## Language Notes

### Orthography
- Multiple transcription systems exist (French-based, English-based, IPA)
- Tonal marking varies: some use accents (kérén), some don't (keren)
- Special characters: ɛ, ɔ, ŋ, ɲ
- Tone is phonemic but often unmarked in casual transcription

### Grammar Key Points
- SOV word order
- Plural formed by adding -e suffix
- No noun classes or grammatical gender
- No definite/indefinite articles
- Negation with particle "mu"
- Wh-in-situ (question words stay in place)
- Verbal prefixes mà- (multiplicative) and rà- (causative)

### Dialects
- Guinea coastal (main, Conakry area)
- Sierra Leone variant
- Approximately 2.4-3 million speakers
