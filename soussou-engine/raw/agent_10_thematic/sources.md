# Soussou Engine - Agent 10 Thematic Vocabulary Sources

## Collection Date
2025-11-20

## Summary
This collection contains **98 validated Susu vocabulary entries** organized into 13 thematic categories: numbers, colors, body parts, animals, food, nature, family, people, common nouns, greetings, useful phrases, pronouns, and verbs.

## Primary Sources

### 1. Glosbe English-Susu Dictionary
**URL:** https://glosbe.com/en/sus
**Type:** Online dictionary
**Reliability:** High (community-verified translations)
**Contributor:** Rajki Andras

**Vocabulary extracted:**
- Body parts: xunyi (head), ya (eye), tuli (ear), nywe (nose), de (mouth), nyinyi (tooth), yi (hand), sanyi (foot)
- Animals: bare (dog), nyari (cat), ninge (cow), si (goat), toxe (chicken), xoni (bird), swe (horse), yexe (fish), senyaxi (snake)
- Food: ye (water), male (rice), sube (meat)
- Nature: soge (sun), kike (moon), lolwe (star), wuri (tree), tune (rain), te (fire)
- Family: baba (father), n'ga (mother), di (child)
- People: adama/xame (man), gine (woman), bore/funye (friend)
- Other: wuli (blood), xili (name), kira (road), kobri (money)
- Colors: fixe (white), fore (black)

### 2. Languages and Numbers - Susu Counting
**URL:** https://www.languagesandnumbers.com/how-to-count-in-susu/en/sus/
**Type:** Educational resource
**Reliability:** High (specialized language resource)

**Vocabulary extracted:**
- Complete number system 1-1000
- Including: keren (1), firin (2), saxan (3), naani (4), suli (5), senni (6), solofere (7), solomasaxan (8), solomanaani (9), fuu (10)
- Tens formation: moxogen (20), tongo + multiplier (30-90)
- Higher numbers: keme (100), wulu (1000)
